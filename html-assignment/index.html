About Me

Welcome to my 1st website! I enjoy creating things with my hands, such as knitting bags, knitting dolls, making figures, and crafting miniatures. For that reason, I initially wanted to pursue Baking and Pastry Arts Management at Algonquin College. However, all of sudden, IRCC's policy changed, and if I major in baking, I won't be eligible for a work permit after graduation.

After consulting with a school advisor and exploring various option, I decided to apply for the IMD. While it's different from handcrafting, I find it fascinating to create something creative with just my fingertips. Although everything feels new and unfamiliar, I'm ready to learn and grow in this field. Thank you.
